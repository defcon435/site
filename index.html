what is dc435

  • dc435 is an official defcon group in cache valley, ut focused on infosec
  • dc435 is made up of students, professionals, researchers, and hobbyists
  • dc435 is always free and open to everyone regardless of skill, age, career, gender, etc.
  • dc435 is community-driven and all events are donated through time from members
  • if you want to present or request topics, post in preso_discussion on discord
